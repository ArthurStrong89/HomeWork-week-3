War Thunder: one of the best games

The War Thunder is one of the greatest games to try your virtual pilot's skills and to have a fun with friends.
It's because of it's realism, action and the adrenaline, that takes it's top during the process.
In WT you can use three types of vechicles: marine, air and ground.
Here we will talk about some tactickal and strategic trics that can help you to win.

Fighters' attack...
Fast and Furious!!!!

Huawowooow!!!
That' was a hard nut!!!!...
Some times I can see frases like this one after a fighter bits a big bird, such as bomber and assault plane.
Fighters have their special advantages, that let you make some tricky tactick of fighting.
For example, Russian ones have very high speed, manuverity and thrust rate, big and deadly guns, etc.
But they also have their disadvantages, such as small streigth of construction and less than other-planes-guns-magazines.
Those things make them very dangerous in a dogfight, ground attack and bombers' hunting.
British planes have a lot of ammo in guns' magazines, but they're slowlier and have less manuverity in the game.
And they are very dangerous in front attack.
You can read more about the characteristics of every plane here
